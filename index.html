About Us

Technology-Driven Farming Built for Long-Term Growth

Rethorium Farm is an innovative agricultural venture developed on a two-acre parcel of land with favourable topography, suitable climate conditions, and a rich soil profile. These strengths were assessed and validated by GeoPard Agriculture, confirming the land's strong capacity for sustainable and intensive farming operations over the long term.


As part of the broader Rethorium Group platform, the farm is built to revolutionize agriculture through modern, intelligent, and technology-driven practices led by Nigerian youth. We combine practical agricultural frameworks with scalable, efficient, and data-informed farming methods to increase productivity while managing resources responsibly.


Our focus is extensive crop production and greenhouse farming, allowing us to serve markets consistently while improving food availability, creating jobs, and contributing to economic growth across West Africa.

  • We are building a sustainable agribusiness model that combines innovation, youth empowerment, and strategic farm management.
  • Our mission is to address the long-standing neglect of agriculture by proving that smart farming can be profitable, scalable, and community-focused.
  • Rethorium Farm is positioned as a practical model for modern agribusiness with strong potential for long-term expansion.
